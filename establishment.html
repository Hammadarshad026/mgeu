Establishment

Establishment

The pivotal role of Establishment within MGEU encompasses a diverse range of functions crucial for laying the groundwork and facilitating operational excellence. Beginning with the formulation of MGEU's visionary objectives, mission, and values, Establishment ensures unwavering adherence to legal mandates and regulatory frameworks. It meticulously structures MGEU's operational architecture, optimizing resource allocation and crafting robust policies and procedures to navigate organizational pathways effectively.

Moreover, Establishment diligently nurtures a culture of positivity within MGEU, adeptly managing stakeholder relations and spearheading strategic planning endeavors to realize enduring objectives. Through its holistic approach, Establishment serves as the bedrock upon which MGEU thrives and excels in its noble pursuits. It's imperative to underscore that each individual contributing to MGEU's noble cause does so voluntarily, selflessly investing their time and expertise to foster global betterment and champion a sustainable future for all.

The Establishment is composed of two primary sections:

  1. Global Heads:

    • Royal Imperial Command: The supreme governing body, overseeing all operations with unparalleled authority and wisdom.

    • National Dominion Command: A network of three key officers in each of the 170+ countries, ensuring the effective execution of MGEU's mission on a national level.

  2. Divisions:

    • 18 specialized divisions, each led by a Director General and supported by a dedicated team, focused on specific areas of MGEU's mission and objectives. Each division embodies its unique identity through a distinctive logo, symbolizing its commitment to excellence within the broader framework of MGEU's vision and mission.

Through this meticulously structured Establishment, the MGEU is poised to achieve its noble aspirations and foster a sustainable future for all.


1-Global Heads

The Global Heads section of the MGEU represents the highest echelon of leadership and governance within the organization. It is meticulously designed to ensure seamless operations and effective implementation of MGEU's mission and objectives. This section is divided into two primary components: the Royal Imperial Command and the National Dominion Command .

Royal Imperial Command

At the zenith of MGEU's organizational hierarchy resides the esteemed Royal Imperial Command, an assembly of paramount authority and indomitable leadership. Nestled within the heart of MGEU's headquarters in the United States of America, this sovereign section orchestrates the grand symphony of our global dominion with unwavering resolve and unparalleled sagacity.

Led by the illustrious OWNER / Chief Commanding and Controlling General / President / Principal Steward, alongside the distinguished Chief Secretary-General of all Divisions, known as the Noble General, this exalted cadre commands the entirety of MGEU's operations with regal finesse and sovereign acumen. Assisted by the Secretary General of all Divisions, also holding the esteemed title of Noble General, and the Joint Chief Commanding and Controlling General, this formidable command structure ensures the seamless execution of our noble mission across continents.

With the sage counsel of the Special Deputies to the Chief Commanding and Controlling General and the Joint Chief Commanding and Controlling General, this august assembly remains steadfast in safeguarding MGEU's legacy of excellence and global impact.

Bearing witness to their majestic stewardship, the Royal Imperial Command stands as the pinnacle of authority within MGEU, guiding our collective journey towards a future adorned with triumph and enlightenment.

  • Chief Commanding and Controlling General

  • Chief Secretary-General of all Divisions - Noble General (Headquarters in the United States of America)

  • Secretary General of all Divisions - Noble General

  • Joint Chief Commanding and Controlling General

  • Special Deputy to the Chief Commanding and Controlling General

  • Special Deputy to the Joint Chief Commanding and Controlling General

National Dominion Command

The National Dominion Command stands as a testament to MGEU’s unwavering commitment to excellence and sovereign governance across the globe. This illustrious body comprises three pivotal officers from each of the 170 countries in which MGEU operates, ensuring the seamless execution of our noble mission worldwide.

At the helm of each nation's operations is the Country Director-General, a distinguished leader entrusted with supreme authority over their respective country. Under their command are the Country Director and Country Deputy Director, forming a triad of leadership that epitomizes dedication, loyalty, and unparalleled service to MGEU’s vision.

The Country Director-General is the chief custodian of MGEU’s interests within their nation, bearing the weighty responsibility of reporting directly to the Royal Imperial Command. This communication is conducted either through direct channels or via the esteemed MGEU High Commissioners / Ambassadors stationed in each country. High Commissioners / Ambassadors, in turn, maintain a direct reporting line to the Director General of the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division, ensuring a cohesive and well-orchestrated diplomatic effort.

The Country Director and Country Deputy Director serve diligently under the guidance of the Country Director-General, with their duties encompassing the support and execution of MGEU’s strategic objectives at the national level. These officers report directly to their Country Director-General and maintain a crucial link to the Director General of the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division, thereby ensuring a unified approach to MGEU’s international endeavors.

The allocation of High Commissioners / Ambassadors to all countries, spanning over 170 nations, reflects MGEU’s commitment to fostering smooth operations and robust international relations through the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division. While the High Commissioners / Ambassadors play a pivotal role in diplomacy, the overarching command and strategic direction remain firmly with the Royal Imperial Command, ensuring that MGEU’s principles and directives are upheld with regal authority and precision.

The National Dominion Command symbolizes the pinnacle of national leadership within MGEU, a beacon of our global prowess and steadfast governance, perpetually guided by the august Royal Imperial Command. This section operates exclusively under the directives of the Royal Imperial Command and is directly connected to and responsible for reporting to both the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division of MGEU and the Royal Imperial Command.

  • Country Director-General (From each country around the globe)

  • Country Director (From each country around the globe)

  • Country Deputy Director (From each country around the globe)

As a distinguished role within the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division of the MGEU, the position of High Commissioner (HC) bears significant responsibility. Reporting directly to both the Chief Commanding and Controlling General (CCCG) of the MGEU and the Director General of the Foreign Affairs and Global Relations Division, the HC is entrusted with advancing the 20 missionary goals of the MGEU through strategic international engagements.The primary objective of the HC is to diligently foster and strengthen relations in alignment with the overarching mission of the MGEU, focusing particularly on a designated country. This involves maintaining consistent communication and engagement with officials of the designated country, aimed at bolstering ties between them and the MGEU in pursuit of its missionary goals.Furthermore, the HC serves as a representative of the MGEU's mission and interests, actively identifying collaboration opportunities and facilitating cultural exchange initiatives. In the capacity of a key liaison, the HC earnestly endeavors to promote cooperation and mutual understanding between the MGEU and the assigned country, thereby contributing significantly to the fulfillment of MGEU's 20 missionary goals."
